Django Unchained

Director: Quentin Tarantino

Opis: Django Unchained, directed by Quentin Tarantino, follows Django, a freed slave, who partners with Dr. King Schultz, a bounty hunter, in pre-Civil War America. As they travel together capturing outlaws, Schultz helps Django in his quest to rescue his wife, Broomhilda, from the brutal plantation owner Calvin Candie. Blending intense action with Tarantino’s signature dark humor and stylistic dialogue, the film explores themes of revenge, freedom, and justice, set against the harsh realities of slavery. Known for its bold storytelling, memorable characters, and powerful performances, Django Unchained challenges and entertains audiences with its unflinching look at American history.

Utisak: Django Unchained je Tarantinov film koji je zaista ostavio jak utisak na mene. Priča prati Djangoa, oslobođenog roba koji zajedno s lovcem na ucene dr. Kingom Schultzom kreće u potragu za svojom suprugom, zarobljenom na surovom imanju Calvina Candieja. Tarantino je, u svom prepoznatljivom stilu, spojio intenzivnu akciju, tamni humor i dijaloge koji drže pažnju od prve do poslednje sekunde.
