Edge of Tomorrow

Director: Doug Liman

Opis: Edge of Tomorrow is a sci-fi action film that follows Major William Cage (Tom Cruise), a public relations officer with no combat experience who is thrust into the front lines of a war against alien invaders known as Mimics. After dying in battle, Cage finds himself stuck in a time loop, forced to relive the same brutal day over and over. Each reset allows him to improve his skills, learning how to survive longer each time. Alongside war hero Rita Vrataski (Emily Blunt), Cage must use his new abilities to uncover the secret to defeating the aliens and ending the invasion.

Utisak: Edge of Tomorrow je uzbudljiv film koji kombinuje akciju i naučnu fantastiku na veoma zanimljiv način. Priča prati vojnika Williama Cagea (Tom Cruise), koji se, nakon što je ubijen u borbi protiv vanzemaljaca, suočava s vremenskom petljom – ponovo proživljava isti dan. Ovaj koncept je genijalan jer omogućava razne mogućnosti razvoja likova i dinamiku koja drži pažnju.
